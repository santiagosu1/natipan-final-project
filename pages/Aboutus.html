History

Natipan began as a bakery with a display window in 1998, never imagining that this dream would become a staple food business in homes across Antioquia, the Coffee Region, and Bogotá, where we now distribute our delicious and varied products.

What started as a one-person livelihood has grown into what we are today: 400 employees, 3 branches, and over 100 vehicles that deliver our products to stores, mini-markets, and supermarkets, ensuring you always find them fresh.

We dream of continuing to delight you with our flavor and variety, a perfect companion for all your meals.

We are Natipan

The company that makes and sells traditional bakery products for family tables. We have four product lines to accompany your best moments: breads, pastries, sweets, and arepas.

Mission

We are a company dedicated to the production and marketing of food products perfect for accompanying family meals.

Vision

We will bring our different bakery products to every corner of the country and be recognized as the leading bakery in varied foods for the family.

Social Responsibility

In accordance with our brand philosophy and the values ​​that guide us as a company, we contribute to various foundations to support their causes and give back to society a little of what it has given us, allowing us to grow as a company.

Our efforts are also focused on our team, prioritizing their well-being. That's why we have programs designed to foster family time with celebrations, recreational vacations, and sunny days out. Your families are as important to us as you are!
